Use & care

Clean head. Charged device. Soft pressure.

The Electric Gua Sha is built for gentle, repeatable use. Keep the head clean, charge before long sessions, and always use as directed.

Before use

Start with clean skin. Wipe the head with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Apply a lightweight serum if you usually use one.

During use

Use light pressure, slow strokes. Avoid pulling at the skin. Avoid broken, irritated or uncomfortable skin entirely. Stop use if discomfort occurs.

After use

Power off, wipe the head clean with a soft cloth and let it air-dry before returning it to its drawstring pouch.

Charging & storage

Charge with the included USB cable as needed. Store in the pouch, away from direct heat or moisture. Do not submerge the device.

Personal-use safety note

Use only as directed in the included manual. Do not use on broken, irritated, inflamed or uncomfortable skin. Do not share personal-use tools. Stop use if discomfort occurs. If unsure, consult a qualified skincare professional.
