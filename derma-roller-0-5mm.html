Use & care

Clean tool. Clean skin. Clean storage.

Derma rollers are personal-use tools. They work best inside a careful, consistent care routine. Always use as directed and follow the steps below.

Before use

Wash hands. Cleanse skin and dry. Sanitize the roller and let it air-dry before it touches your skin.

During use

Use light pressure with slow, controlled passes. Do not press hard. Avoid broken, irritated, inflamed or uncomfortable skin. Stop immediately if anything feels wrong.

After use

Sanitize the roller again, rinse, and let it dry fully. Return it to the included clear holder for storage. Keep the holder dry between uses.

Personal-use safety note

Use only as directed. Do not use on broken, irritated, inflamed or uncomfortable skin. Do not share personal-use tools. Stop use if discomfort occurs. If unsure, consult a qualified skincare professional.

Frequently asked

Good to know before you start.

How often can I use the 0.5mm roller?

Use only as directed in the included guidance. Start conservatively, build a calm cadence over time, and always leave appropriate recovery time between sessions.

What areas is it designed for?

The 0.5mm head is suited to face, beard line and scalp ritual use. Always avoid broken, irritated or uncomfortable skin, no matter the area.

How do I clean and store it?

Sanitize before and after each use, dry fully, and return it to the included clear storage holder. Keep the storage holder dry between uses.

Should I start with 0.5mm or 1.5mm?

Start with 0.5mm if you're new. The 1.5mm is intended for experienced users who already know their pace.
